frequently asked questions

        

            
how do i clean my csv file?

            
upload your csv file or paste data directly. our tool will detect issues like duplicates and missing values, then clean your spreadsheet instantly.

        

        

            
is my data private when i clean spreadsheets?

            
files are sent to our server, processed in memory, and returned to you. nothing is written to disk, logged, or shared with any third party. for the full details, see the privacy policy.

        

        

            
can i remove duplicates from my spreadsheet?

            
yes. our duplicate remover identifies and removes duplicate rows while keeping the first occurrence.

        

        

            
do i need to sign up to clean data?

            
no sign-up required. this free online data cleaning tool works immediately without registration.
